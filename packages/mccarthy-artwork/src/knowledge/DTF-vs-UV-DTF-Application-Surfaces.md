# DTF vs UV DTF: Application Surface Guide

## DTF (Direct-to-Film) Transfers

### Suitable For: ALL TEXTILES

DTF transfers are designed exclusively for fabric and textile-based materials. The adhesive powder and heat press process creates a flexible, washable bond with textile fibers.

#### Compatible Surfaces:

- ✅ **Cotton** - 100% cotton t-shirts, hoodies, tote bags
- ✅ **Poly** - Polyester sportswear, performance fabrics, flags
- ✅ **Blends** - Cotton/poly blends, tri-blends, and other fabric combinations
- ✅ **Canvas** - Canvas bags, shoes, art panels
- ✅ **Hessian** - Burlap, jute bags, rustic textile products
- ✅ **Leather** - Genuine leather jackets, bags, accessories
- ✅ **Leatherette** - Synthetic leather, PU leather, vegan leather products
- ✅ **Denim** - Jeans, denim jackets, denim accessories
- ✅ **Neoprene** - Can coolers, laptop sleeves, wetsuits, protective gear
- ✅ **Wood** - Fabric-covered or textile-laminated wood surfaces
- ✅ **MDF** - Fabric-covered or textile-laminated MDF surfaces
- ✅ **Cardboard** - Fabric-covered or textile-laminated cardboard surfaces

### Application Method:
Heat press application with controlled temperature, pressure, and time settings specific to each fabric type.

---

## UV DTF (UV Direct-to-Film) Stickers

### Suitable For: HARD SURFACES ONLY

UV DTF creates durable, waterproof stickers with a clear laminate layer that adheres to non-porous hard surfaces. These are NOT suitable for textiles.

#### Compatible Surfaces:

- ✅ **Metal** - Stainless steel tumblers, aluminum bottles, tin signs, metal surfaces
- ✅ **Glass** - Glassware, windows, mirrors, glass bottles, jars
- ✅ **Wood** - Painted or sealed wood, wooden signs, furniture
- ✅ **Ceramic** - Mugs, plates, tiles, ceramic drinkware
- ✅ **Plastic** - Hard plastic containers, phone cases, promotional items
- ✅ **Acrylic** - Acrylic signs, displays, awards, keychains
- ✅ **PVC** - PVC products, signage, hard surfaces
- ✅ **& Much More** - Any smooth, non-porous hard surface

### Application Method:
Peel-and-stick application. The UV DTF sticker is simply peeled from its backing and applied to the clean, dry surface. No heat press required.

---

## Key Differences Summary

| Feature | DTF Transfers | UV DTF Stickers |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|
| **Surface Type** | Textiles & Fabrics Only | Hard Surfaces Only |
| **Application** | Heat Press Required | Peel & Stick |
| **Flexibility** | Flexible, moves with fabric | Rigid sticker |
| **Washability** | Washing machine safe | Dishwasher safe (top rack) |
| **Best For** | Apparel, bags, textile products | Drinkware, signage, hard goods |

---

## ❌ Important Notes

- **DTF transfers will NOT work** on hard, non-porous surfaces like metal, glass, or plastic
- **UV DTF stickers will NOT work** on textiles, fabrics, or porous surfaces
- Always choose the correct technology for your substrate to ensure proper adhesion and durability
